Value Investing — a personal equity analysis platform

What is this?

This platform is a personal toolkit for applying value investing principles to equity analysis. It draws on the frameworks of Warren Buffett and Benjamin Graham — focusing on intrinsic value, margin of safety, and long-term fundamentals rather than short-term price movements.

Warren's Rules
  • 1
    Vigilant Leaders
    Debt/Equity < 0.5, Current Ratio 1.5–2.5, ROE > 8%, adequate Dividend Cover.
  • 2
    Long Term Prospects
    A durable competitive advantage and a clear, understandable business model.
  • 3
    Stable & Understandable
    Consistent EPS, growing equity per share, reliable dividends, and growing capital employed.
  • 4
    Undervalued
    P/E < 15, Price/Book < 1.5, DCF intrinsic value above current share price, adequate margin of safety.
Magic Formula

The Magic Formula (Joel Greenblatt) ranks companies by two metrics: Return on Capital Employed (ROCE > 7%) and Earnings Yield. Companies that score well on both criteria simultaneously represent statistically superior investment candidates.
